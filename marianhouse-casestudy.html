Marian House Website ReDesign Case Study

Role: UX Designer

Marian House was designed to house and care for women in Baltimore City. The current donation product is not promoting trust through their online presence, which is causing potential donors to opt out of giving to Marian House. By promoting trust and elevating transparency, users will increase monetary donations to Mariar House.
